Welcome to the Purdue Math Course Helper!

We understand that math can be overwhemling and often hard to manage. Tt can feel like you're being left behind even if you only missed one class.

That's why this website exists to help you keep track of all available math lectures for your class.

Missing a class for a club? Slept through an early class? Simply need a new perspective?

Then come check out our website!

First, we'll need some information. Don't worry, we won't sell your data--promise! :)

Please read this before continuing!

Please enter your class number into the box below. For example, if you're in Calc 3 type in 26100.

Then put in your preferred professors name, make sure to capitalize! If you don't prefer a professor, please write "none".

Finally type in your preferred days of the week as "MWF" for Monday-Wednesday-Friday or "TR" for Tuesday-Thursday classes.

Don't forget to press submit when you are done!
